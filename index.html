About

"Self-proclaimed fashion addict with something to say about it."

I'm a first-year MA (Hons) Business with Marketing student at the University of Edinburgh — and a writer, creator, and someone who thinks deeply about the intersection of fashion, culture, and commerce.

From running my own Substack, Thoughts of a Fashion Addict, to publishing in the student press, hosting student radio, and working in retail and social media management — I bring ideas to life across formats. I believe fashion is a lens through which to read the world.
